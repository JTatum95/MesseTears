Event Details

Dress Code

Please come dressed for a formal afternoon tea/coffee style event. This is not a black tie event, but we do expect everyone to look nice for the occasion. We recommend suit jackets or dress shirts with ties as well as tea-length dresses and nice jumpsuits.

Ceremony

We are going to be married at our home church, Church of the Resurrection. We will have a small in person ceremony as well as a virtual option as needed.

Reception

After the ceremony, we will have a reception time to chat and snack. We will have light refreshments as well as coffee and tea. Following the reception there will be more time to stay if you would wish and we will play board games.

What To Expect

Full Anglican Wedding Service

We will be participating in a full Anglican Wedding Service. This will last roughly an hour and half and will include a brief homily as well a time for Eucharist. The service will be livestreamed to accommodate our remote guests!
